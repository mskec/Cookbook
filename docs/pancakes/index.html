Pancakes

Recipe by Martin Skec from Berlin, Germany

Ingredients

2 people

  • 2 eggs
  • 1.5 cup milk (340 g)
  • 1 cup flour (140 g)
  • 1 Tbsp. oil
  • 1/3 Tbsp. salt

Instructions

  • In a bowl add 2 eggs, milk and oil
  • Mix together for 20 seconds
  • Add flour, salt
  • Mix all until smooth
