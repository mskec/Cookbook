Spicy Pork Tenderloin

Recipe by Chris Morocco from New York, USA

Ingredients

  • 140g pork tenderloin (ribica)
  • 90g jasmin rice
  • Korean chilli paste
  • 3/4 Tbsp. brown sugar
  • 2 Tbsp. soy sauce
  • 1/2 Tbsp. sesame oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1" ginger
  • Sesame seeds
  • Salt

Instructions

Inspiration: Spicy Pork Tenderloin Bowl Recipe

Pro tip

Take out the meat from the bag before cooking it. This will avoid spilling marinade all over the counter top.
