# Leek stew

## Ingredients
* 1 leek
* 2-3 medium potatoes
* 2 carrots
* Olive oil
* Vegeta
* 0.5l water
* 300g of pumpkin soup

## Instructions
### Preparation
* Cut leek in 1.5 finger lengths
* Cut potatoes into bite-sized cubes
* Cut carrot in circles

### In a medium pot
* cover with olive oil
* add leek and carrots
* season with vegeta
* fry for 10mins
* add potatoes
* add a small pot of water
* cook for 40 mins
* add 300g of pumpkin soup
